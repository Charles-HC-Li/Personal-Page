My Background:

I come from Changsha, Hunan 🌶️, a city known as the "Spice Capital of China." I spent the first 18 years of my life there, and it left a lasting impression. Changsha is a city full of vitality 🎆, with the air filled with the delightful aroma of spicy cuisine. People here are warm-hearted ❤️, although sometimes lacking in manners 😅, their authenticity fascinates me.

Whenever I think of Changsha, I reminisce about the delicious local dishes like stinky tofu 🍢, chopped chili fish head 🐟, and spicy stir-fried rice cakes 🍲. These mouthwatering foods not only tantalize the taste buds but also evoke a deep sense of nostalgia for my hometown. Changsha's streets are always bustling with lively crowds 👥, and the neon lights at night add to the city's charm ✨.

My Hobbies:

My life is filled with a passion for both cuisine and fitness 🍽️💪. Every week, I dedicate time to cooking elaborate meals 🍳 for myself and friends. My favorite culinary styles include Sichuan and Cantonese cuisine, each offering unique flavors and cooking techniques. Whether it's the fiery Sichuan hotpot 🍲 or the light and refreshing Cantonese dishes, I enjoy creating culinary delights in the kitchen.

On the other hand, I'm also a sports enthusiast 🏋️‍♂️, but I don't limit myself to one particular sport. I view various sports as "skills" to be acquired, and each sport provides an opportunity for continuous learning and improvement. In a single week, you might find me trying my hand at basketball 🏀, swimming 🏊‍♂️, yoga 🧘‍♀️, and hitting the gym 🏋️‍♀️. Health and fitness play a significant role in my life, helping me maintain vitality and balance ⚖️.

My Fun Facts:

Here's an incredible fun fact about me 🤯: I once managed to shed a remarkable sixty pounds in just four months! It was a challenging journey, but it stands as a significant achievement in my life, teaching me the importance of perseverance and discipline 🏆.

Another interesting fact is that I had never considered coming to the United States 🌎 before. The opportunity arose in my life, leading me to embrace new challenges and opportunities 🌟, enhancing my adaptability to different cultures 🌍.

Despite my deep love for cats 🐱, I've never owned one. Sometimes, I even experience allergies 🤧, preventing me from getting too close to these adorable creatures. Nevertheless, I'm still drawn to their cuteness and independence 😻.

Once upon a time, I had a dream of teaching in the remote mountains for half a year 🏞️, making a positive impact on underprivileged children. However, as I grew older, I realized that dedicating six months became a greater sacrifice ⏳, and life seemed to be constantly moving forward, much like a wheel we are compelled to chase 🚗. Despite this, I still aspire to contribute positively to society and the community in other ways 🌱.
